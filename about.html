In our course we paint with fluid acrylic paint, also called fluid art.

You will learn different techniques that are used in fluid art and how to mix up the paints with different pouring-mediums and to different consistencies.


The course are based on pre-reccorded video lessons.

You will recive an email, after registration, with the link to our platform where you find all the videos, pouring-medium recipies and our online community.


Embellish your dry paintings.

Sometimes you feel that your paining isn't finnished when it's dried. Therefore we offer an addition to the course with embellishments.

Here you will get some ideas how to embellish your work and also some tips and tricks along the way.


Paint with us offers four opptions of this course

Online with feedback from the tutor

Online without feedback from the tutor

Online with embellishments, with feedback from the tutor

Online with embellishments, without feedback from the tutor
